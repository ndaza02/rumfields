About Mining Industrial Lubricant Suppliers (MILS)

MILS is a registered lubricant and oil company headquartered in Bulawayo, Zimbabwe. We are involved in the supply of petroleum-based lubricants and grease products, including blended Motor oils, Brake fluids, and Lubricating grease.

As a proud TotalEnergies Authorised Distributor, we are committed to providing world-class lubricant solutions for every sector. From heavy-duty mining and industrial applications to passenger vehicles and agriculture, MILS is your trusted source for quality, reliability, and performance.

We are set to service a wide range of clientele not only in and around Zimbabwe but also throughout the SADC region.

Our Mission: To establish a world-class Oil and lubricant Supplies Company whose products will not only be retailed in Zimbabwe, but also exported to other parts of Africa.

Our Vision: To become one of the leading brands in the lubricant oil business in Zimbabwe.
